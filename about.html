About Us

Google Developer Student Club KLEF, Vijayawada is inspired by Google Developers Family. We started our journey in Aug 2018. We are a group of passionate students who try to engage fellow student developers through our events, workshops, codelabs and meetups. The motive is to create a local ecosystem of programmers & developers in and around the Campus.
